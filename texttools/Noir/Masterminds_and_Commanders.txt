#FIGHT: Each other player <b>Investigates</b> their deck for a card with an Attack icon and KOs it. Players reveal all the cards they <b>Investigated</b>.

#TACTIC: Corrupt Weak Minds
#FIGHT: Each other player puts a random Bystander from their Victory Pile onto a Hero in the HQ as a <b>Hidden Witness</b>.

#LEADS: Goblin's Freak Show
#STRIKE: Two random Bystanders from each player's Victory Pile become <b>Hidden Witnesses</b> held by The Goblin. Any player who didn't have two Bystanders gains a Wound instead.
#ATTACK: 10*
#VP: 6

#TACTIC: Sinister Dreams
#FIGHT: Each other player reveals a Sinister Six or Spider-Friends Hero or gains a Wound.

#TACTIC: Blackmail the Judges
#FIGHT: For each Goblin's Freak Show Villain in the city, The Goblin captures 2 <b>Hidden Witnesses</b>.

#TACTIC: Carnival of Carnage
#FIGHT: For each Goblin's Freak Show Villain in the city, each other player discards a card.

#TACTIC: Blind Loyalty
#FIGHT: <b>Investigate</b> the Villain Deck for a Goblin's Freak Show Villain and put it into your Victory Pile.

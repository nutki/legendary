
#CARDNAME: Ruin the Perfect Wedding
#SETUP: 11 Twists. Set aside two extra Heroes to get married. Prepare each Wedding Hero into a seperate 14-card stack, ordered by cost with the lowest cost on top.
#TWISTNR: NR[1] Put one Wedding Hero Stack above the rightmost city space "at the altar." Gain its top card.
#TWISTNR: NR[2] Put the other Wedding Hero Stack above the Mastermind space "at the door." Gain its top card.
#TWISTNR: NR[3-7] Gain the top card of either Wedding Hero Stack. Then KO two cards from the top of each Wedding Hero Stack that has a Villain or Mastermind in the space immediately below it. Then the leftmost Hero Stack "walks down the aisle," moving one space to the right.
#TWISTNR: NR[8-11] KO two cards from the top of each Wedding Hero Stack.
#EVILWINS: When either Wedding Hero Stack is KO'd.

#CARDNAME: War of Kings
#SETUP: 11 Twists.
#TWISTNR: NR[1-8] Stack this Twist next to the Scheme as a "Battlefront." This turn, you may pay 1 Recruit per Battlefront to supply the war.
<ul>
    <li> <b>If you pay</b>: You gain the {THRONES FAVOR}. You may KO one of your cards.</li>
    <li> <b>If you don't pay by the end of turn</b>: Right after you draw a new hand, stack a card from the S.H.I.E.L.D. Officer Stack next to the Scheme as a "Victorious General." The Mastermind gains the {THRONES FAVOR}. If they aleady have it, you gain a Wound.</li>
</ul>

#TWISTNR: NR[9-11] Same effect, but with two Victorious Generals.
#EVILWINS: When there are 6 Victorious Generals.

#CARDNAME: Tornado of Terrigen Mists
#SETUP: 10 Twists. Each player puts a small object above the sewers to represent themself.
#RULE: You can't fight Villains outside the city space where you are. (You can still recruit from all HQ spaces and fight the Mastermind.) During your turn, you can spend 1 Attack any number of times to move yourself one space left or right.
#TWISTNR: NR[1] Put this Tornado Scheme card above the Sewers.
#TWISTNR: NR[2-5] Each player in the Tornado space gains a Wound. Then move this Tornado card and each Villain simultaneously one space to the left. (A Villain on the Bridge escapes.)
#TWISTNR: NR[6-9] Same effect, but move them all to the right, if possible. (A Villain in the Sewers doesn't move.)
#TWISTNR: NR[10] Evil Wins!

#CARDNAME: Devolve with Xerogen Crystals
#SETUP: Add Twists equal to the number of players plus 3. Add an extra Henchman Group of 10 cards as "Xerogen Experiments."
#RULE: All Xerogen Experiments also have {ABOMINATION}.
#TWIST: Choose a Hero in the HQ that doesn't have a printed Attack of 2 or more. Put it on the bottom of the Hero Deck. Then play two cards from the Villain Deck.
#EVILWINS: When there are 3 Villains per player in the Escape Pile or the Villain Deck runs out.

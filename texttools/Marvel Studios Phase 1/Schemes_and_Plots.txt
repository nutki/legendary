
#CARDNAME: Asgard Under Siege
#SETUP: 8 Twists. Add an extra Henchman group to the Villain Deck.
#TWIST: Play the top 2 cards of the Villain Deck.
#EVILWINS: If 12 Villains escape.

#CARDNAME: Destroy the Cities of Earth!
#SETUP: 8 Twists. 12 Bystanders in the Villain Deck.
#RULE: Each Villain gets +1 Attack for each Bystander it has.
#TWIST: Any Villain in the Bank captures 2 Bystanders. Then play the top card of the Villain Deck.
#EVILWINS: When 8 Bystanders are carried away by escaping Villains.

#CARDNAME: Enslave Minds with the Chitauri Scepter
#SETUP: 8 Twists. 6 Heroes. Chitauri Villain Group required. Shuffle 12 random Heroes from the Hero Deck into the Villain Deck.
#RULE: Heroes in the Villain Deck count as "Enslaved" Villains with Attack equal to the Hero's Cost +2. If you defeat that Hero, you gain it.
#TWIST: The highest-cost Hero from the HQ moves into the Sewers as an "Enslaved" Villain, as above.
#EVILWINS: If 6 Heroes get into the Escaped Villains pile.

#CARDNAME: Invade Asgard
#SETUP: 7 Twists. Each Twist is a Dark Portal.
#TWISTNR: NR[1] Put the Dark Portal above the Mastermind. The Mastermind gets +1 Attack.
#TWISTNR: NR[2-6] Put the Dark Portal in the leftmost city space that doesn't yet have a Dark Portal. Villains in that city space get +1 Attack.
#TWISTNR: NR[7] Evil Wins!

#CARDNAME: Radioactive Palladium Poisoning
#SETUP: 8 Twists. Wound stack holds 6 Wounds per player.
#TWIST: Each player reveals a [Tech] Hero or gains a Wound.
#EVILWINS: If the Wound stack runs out.

#CARDNAME: Replace Earth's Leaders with HYDRA
#SETUP: 5 Twists. 3 additional Twists next to this Scheme. 18 total Bystanders in the Villain Deck.
#RULE: Bystanders in the Villain Deck count as "Infiltrator" Villains, with Attack equal to the number of Twists next to this Scheme.
#TWIST: Put the Twist next to this Scheme.
#EVILWINS: If 5 "Infiltrators" escape.

#CARDNAME: Super Hero Civil War
#SETUP: 2: Use 4 Heroes in the Hero Deck. 2-3 players: 8 Twists. 4-5 players: 5 Twists.
#TWIST: KO all the Heroes in the HQ.
#EVILWINS: If the Hero Deck runs out.

#CARDNAME: Unleash the Power of the Cosmic Cube
#SETUP: 8 Twists.
#TWIST: Put the Twist next to this Scheme.
#TWISTNR: NR[5-6] Each player gains a Wound.
#TWISTNR: NR[7] Each player gains 3 Wounds.
#TWISTNR: NR[8] Evil Wins!
